How The App Works?

Sign-Up

Signing up for NIHAMISHE is straightforward and secure. New users can easily create their accounts by providing essential details such as name, email address, and contact information. The process is designed to be quick and intuitive, ensuring that users can start using the app in minutes.

Login

Once registered, users can access their NIHAMISHE accounts through a secure login process. This ensures privacy and security for all user interactions within the app. Logging in grants access to personalized features and settings, allowing customers and drivers to manage their moving requests and schedules efficiently.

Request

NIHAMISHE empowers users to request moving services effortlessly. Whether it's scheduling a local move or planning a long-distance relocation, the app provides a user-friendly interface to input details such as pickup and delivery locations, preferred date and time, and specific moving requirements. This streamlined process ensures that requests are submitted accurately and promptly.

Pay

To facilitate seamless transactions, NIHAMISHE offers a secure payment gateway integrated into the app. Users can conveniently pay for moving services directly through the platform using various payment methods, including credit/debit cards or digital wallets. This ensures transparency and reliability, allowing customers to confirm bookings and drivers to receive timely payments for their services.
